Intro

Hi,there.

My name is Xiaoguo. I am super passionate about data science, deep learning and robotics, and I am a strong believer of life-long learning and knowledge sharing.

During my Ph.D. candidature I was lucky to have collaborated with different labs across the countries and met amazing people with all kinds of brilliant ideas.

There are so many cool innovations emerging from AI and robotics in this century, and they are like unrelenting waves that is going transforming our lives in incredible ways. It's critical that we catch this wave than being left behind and it's my passion is to share my knowledge with enthusiastic like you.

I hope you enjoy the contents I have to offer. VENI, VIDI, VICI!

When I'm not working

Doing a Ph.D. gives me great flexibility in my time schedules and I do all kinds of sports with I'm not beating down on a keyboard or tinkering my robot.

Tennis is my all time favourite. There is pure pleasure when you hammered that ball into your oppenonts backhand corner. On the more gentle side of the sport, I am a tennis coach in our local community. Just like everything else, teaching and mentoring is the best form of human interaction.

Like many others, I love traveling. Wherever I go, I always like look for exciting spots for surfing. There is something peculiar about surfing in that it's a sport where more than 95% of the experience is struggling and there is no guarantee that you gets to pop-up on your board and enjoy the 5% on the wave. That's what I love about it, it's like life.
